Vikram Batra - The Hero of Kargil

Captain Vikram Batra

Captain Vikram Batra was an Indian Army officer who sacrificed his life during the Kargil War in 1999. He was posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest military honor. His last words, "Yeh Dil Maange More!", remain a symbol of bravery and dedication to the nation.

Timeline of Achievements of Vikram Batra

1974 - Born in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh
1996 - Joined the Indian Military Academy
1999 - Led the charge in Kargil War
1999 - Martyred during the Battle of Point 4875
1999 - Awarded Param Vir Chakra posthumously
